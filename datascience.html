Master's thesis

  • I love stand-up comedy and I enjoy giving people new comics to listen to when asked. The problem is that I struggle(d) to recommend people for John Mulaney. He isn't in my purview of enjoyable comics---don't get me wrong because I think he is clever and funny, but I just feel he is selling chuckles and not bellylaughs. However, I like to have answers for people. Subsequently, I want to be able to identify comics are similar to Mulaney and comics who cover similar topics to Mulaney. Hence, I decided to search for transcribed monologues, and thankfully, there is a database out there housed by Scripts From The Loft. Using R, my approach is simple, I parse the "p" nodes, clean the data, token/stem/lemma the data, and make pretty visuals and a nice R Shiny app to showcase the work. Comic recommendation in R (NLP; tokenization, stemming, lemmatization, nonnegative matrix factorization)
